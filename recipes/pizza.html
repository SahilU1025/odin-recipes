Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French bread, halved horizontally
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese (optional)
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place French bread halves on a baking sheet, cut sides up. Mix melted butter with garlic powder and brush over bread.
  3. Toast in the oven for 4 to 5 minutes until lightly golden. Remove and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, combine chicken with Buffalo sauce and ranch dressing.
  5. Spread chicken mixture evenly over toasted bread. Top with mozzarella, cheddar, and blue cheese if using.
  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Remove from oven, sprinkle with green onions, slice, and serve warm.
