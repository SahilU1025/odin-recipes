BBQ Cheesy Chicken Bread

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf Italian bread or French bread, halved lengthwise
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup barbecue sauce (your favorite)
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded smoked Gouda or cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Arrange bread halves cut side up on a baking sheet. Mix melted butter and garlic powder, then brush evenly over bread.
  3. Toast in oven for 5 minutes, then remove.
  4. In a bowl, mix chicken with BBQ sauce until evenly coated. Spread mixture over toasted bread.
  5. Top with sliced red onions, mozzarella, and Gouda.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until cheese is melted and golden brown.
  7. Garnish with chopped cilantro. Slice and serve hot.
